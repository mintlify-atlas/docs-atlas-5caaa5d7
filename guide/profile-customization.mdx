Sable offers extensive profile customization options to help you express yourself in the Matrix network.

## Profile Banners

Added in version 1.1.3, profile banners allow you to set a custom header image for your profile.

### Setting a Profile Banner

1. Open your **User Settings**
2. Navigate to your profile section
3. Click on the banner area to upload an image
4. Your banner will be visible to all users who view your profile

<Note>
Sable supports the Commet profile banner format, ensuring compatibility with other Matrix clients that use the same format.
</Note>

## Pronouns

Sable supports pronoun display following the MSC4247 format. Pronouns appear as small pills next to your display name.

### Adding Pronouns

1. Open **Settings**
2. Navigate to your profile section
3. Enter your pronouns in the pronouns field
4. Save your changes

### Pronoun Display

- **Desktop**: Up to 3 pronoun pills are displayed
- **Mobile**: 1 pronoun pill is displayed to save space

Pronouns are visible in:
- User profiles
- Member lists
- Chat messages (when hovering over usernames)

<Warning>
In version 1.2.1, the pronoun format was updated to better match MSC4247. If you set pronouns before this update, you'll need to reset them.
</Warning>

## Bio Editor

Your bio is a longer text description that appears on your profile page.

### Editing Your Bio

1. Open **Settings**
2. Navigate to your profile section
3. Enter or edit your bio in the bio text field
4. Save your changes

### Bio Features

- **Character limit**: Up to 1,024 characters
- **Formatting support**: Basic text formatting is supported
- **Commet compatibility**: Sable supports bio formats from Commet, another Matrix client

## Display Name and Avatar

### Changing Your Display Name

1. Open **Settings**
2. Navigate to your profile section
3. Enter your new display name
4. Save your changes

Your display name is what other users see across all rooms and spaces.

### Changing Your Avatar

1. Open **Settings**
2. Navigate to your profile section
3. Click on your current avatar
4. Upload a new image
5. Crop and adjust as needed
6. Save your changes

Your avatar appears next to your messages and in member lists throughout Sable.

## Timezones

Sable supports displaying your timezone on your profile, making it easier for others to know when you're likely to be available.

1. Open **Settings**
2. Navigate to your profile section
3. Select your timezone from the dropdown
4. Save your changes
