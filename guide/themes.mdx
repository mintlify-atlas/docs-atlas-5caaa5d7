Sable offers multiple themes to personalize your chat experience, with support for both light and dark color schemes.

## Available Themes

Sable includes the following built-in themes:

### Light Themes

- **Silver** - The default light theme with a soft purple accent color
- **Light** - A clean, bright theme with high contrast

### Dark Themes

- **Dark** - Sable's default dark theme with purple accents
- **Butter** - A warm, yellow-tinted dark theme
- **Rosé Pine** - A cozy theme with muted purple and pink tones
- **Gruvbox** - A retro, warm color scheme inspired by the popular Gruvbox palette
- **Cinny Dark** - The classic Cinny dark theme

## Switching Themes

To change your theme:

1. Open **Settings** by clicking your profile picture
2. Navigate to the **Appearance** tab
3. Select your preferred theme from the theme dropdown
4. The theme will apply immediately

## Dark and Light Mode

Sable supports both light and dark themes. The available themes in the dropdown will depend on your system preferences or manual selection.

You can toggle between light and dark modes using the theme selector in the Appearance settings.

## Twitter-Style Emojis

By default, Sable uses Twitter-style emojis (Twemoji) for consistent emoji rendering across all platforms. This ensures emojis look the same whether you're on Windows, macOS, Linux, or mobile devices.

You can disable Twitter-style emojis in the Appearance settings if you prefer to use your system's native emoji font.
